# Project: Lane & Obstacle Detection (Project MANAS)
## VLSI Engineering Rules
- Always use SystemVerilog (2012+).
- Prioritize synchronous resets and avoid inferred latches.
- When writing modules, include a comment block for 'Inputs', 'Outputs', and 'Assumptions'.
- Preferred Tools: Verilator for linting, GTKWave for debugging.
